Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 04/07/2025

Hey there! Welcome to pcmemoryfinder.com. I know privacy policies can be as exciting as watching paint dry, but I promise to keep this short, sweet, and maybe even a little entertaining.

1. Analytics and Tracking

I use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to keep an eye on how people interact with my single-page site (but not in a creepy way). GA4 helps me understand how visitors scroll, click, and hang around.

How it works:

  • Cookies are used: GA4 may place cookies on your device to understand how the page is used.
  • What’s collected: Things like your (partially anonymized) IP address, browser type, device info, time spent on the page, and where you clicked.
  • Google’s role: Google may process this data according to their Privacy Policy.
  • No personal identifiers: I don't know who you are—just that someone visited and poked around a bit.
  • Opting out: If you'd rather not be tracked at all, you can install the GA opt-out browser add-on.

    3. Data Collection and Usage
