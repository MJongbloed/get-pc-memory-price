Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 03/31/2025

Hey there! Welcome to pcmemoryfinder.com. I know privacy policies can be as exciting as watching paint dry, but I promise to keep this short, sweet, and maybe even a little entertaining.

1. Analytics and Tracking

I use Matomo to keep an eye on my website traffic (but not in a creepy way). Here's how I do it:

  • No cookies: I won't sneak cookies onto your device—if you want cookies, I suggest a bakery.
  • Anonymized IP addresses: Your IP address is scrambled like a secret agent's identity before I store it.
  • Anonymized referrer data: If you came here from another website, I don't really know who sent you—just that you arrived. Like a surprise party, but less surprising.

2. Affiliate Links

I'm part of the Amazon Affiliate Program, which means if you click on an Amazon link and buy something, I may get a tiny commission (at no extra cost to you!). Amazon might set cookies because, well, they like knowing where their customers come from. But that's on them, not me!

3. Data Collection and Usage

I do not collect your personal info unless you hand it over willingly (like filling out a contact form). I use any data I collect just to make this site better, not to stalk you.

4. Third-Party Services

I don't sell or share your data with shady third parties. Actually, I don't share it with anyone unless we absolutely have to (like if the law says so).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correct, or delete any data I may have collected about you. Just shoot me an email at info@accentio.nl, and I'll take care of it—no long, boring forms required.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I might update this policy now and then. If anything major changes, I'll probably make an announcement (or at least not hide it in tiny text).

Questions? Concerns? Just want to say hi? Drop me a line at michel@accentio.nl!
